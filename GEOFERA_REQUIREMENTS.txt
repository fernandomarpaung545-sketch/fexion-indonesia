About:
Established in 2026, GEOFERA is a professional platform dedicated to advancing
subsurface excellence through knowledge sharing, technical collaboration, and
professional development. We bring together geoscientists, formation evaluation
specialists, wellsite geologists, reservoir professionals, and industry leaders
to exchange insights, foster innovation, and build meaningful connections.
By combining regional experience with global best practices, GEOFERA helps
professionals and organizations unlock new opportunities and drive sustainable
growth in the energy industry.

Technical Pillars:
1. Geoscience - Interpret the Earth. Unlock Opportunities.
2. Formation Evaluation - Transform Data into Reservoir Insight.
3. Drilling - Deliver Wells Safely and Efficiently.
4. Upstream Integration - Connect Disciplines. Create Value.

Featured Programs:
